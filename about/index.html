Biography

I am currently working as a Computational Biologist at Artizan Biosciences. Prior Artizan, I was a postdoctoral researcher/ computational biologist with the Agricultural microbiomes group at USDA-ARS- Genomics and Bioinformatics Research Unit and the Department of Microbiology and Cell Science at the University of Florida. I am interested in developing computational and statistical tools to support microbiome-based agriculture.
